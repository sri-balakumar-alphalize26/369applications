HR Leave Request

Employee leave requests with manager approval, and paid / unpaid policy that feeds the salary deduction.

1. Requesting leave

  • Menu: Leave Requests → New. Choose leave type, from/to dates (or half-day) and a reason.
  • Submit for approval; a manager Approves or Rejects. Only approved leave affects pay.

2. Paid vs Unpaid

Menu: Leave Policy Configuration.

  • A leave is paid only when an enabled paid-leave policy with remaining quota grants it (checked per year and per month).
  • With no policy, or Paid Leave off, the leave is unpaid.
Unpaid-leave deduction (when "Enable Unpaid Leave Deduction" is on) = wage / working days in month × unpaid days. The daily rate is rounded so it matches the Employee Report.

3. Stays in sync

Leave deductions depend on the working-days figure from the Office Hours config. Changing that config (or clicking Recompute Late Records) recomputes recent leave requests automatically, so the leave list and the report always agree.

4. In the report

Approved leave shows on the Employee Report as Paid Leave Days / Unpaid Leave Days with the Leave Deduction; unpaid days reduce the final salary (they aren't earned), paid days are earned.
