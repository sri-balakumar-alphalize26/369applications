HR Field Attendance

Attendance for field staff, built from vehicle trips and customer visits — not just office check-ins.

1. What it adds

  • An attendance_source = field flag on the attendance record.
  • A Source Trip and Source Visits (links to vehicle trips & customer visits), with multi-trip support.
  • GPS location captured from the first visit, and an auto-derived Visited Stops display.
  • Trip totals: KM travelled, duration, fuel litres & amount.

2. Mobile app

  • The app uses get_today_field_attendance / create_field_attendance to check in from the field and link the trip/visits.
  • "Today's" window is computed in the Office Timezone, so a late-night check-in lands on the correct day.

3. Late tracking is shared

Field check-ins are ordinary attendance records, so late detection, deductions and waivers work exactly like office attendance — anchored to the Office Timezone. They appear in the Employee Report too.

The Field Attendance list shows the office-time Check In / Check Out, plus trip, visits, location, late time and deduction.

If you arrived late, the Late Reason is required before the record can be saved. Click Enter Late Reason, type why in the popup and Save; once entered the button becomes Update Reason (re-opening with the existing reason), and it disappears after check-out.

4. Office time & trip odometer

  • Office time everywhere: trip Start/End Time in Vehicle Tracking and the Date and Time on Customer Visits now display in the Office Timezone (same anchor as attendance), so they read the same for everyone.
  • Start Km carries over: when you create the next trip of the day, its Start Km is pre-filled from the previous (last ended) trip's End Km for odometer continuity — you can still override it.

5. Menu

Menu: Field Attendance — a dedicated list with search filters (My Records, Late Only, With Deduction, Waived).
